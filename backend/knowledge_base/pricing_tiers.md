# CloudScale AI — Pricing Plans

## Overview

CloudScale AI offers three pricing tiers designed to scale with your business. All plans include a fourteen-day free trial with full feature access and no credit card required.

## Starter Plan — Four Hundred Ninety-Nine Dollars Per Month

Best for startups and small teams managing up to fifty cloud instances.

Includes:

- Up to fifty managed instances across one cloud provider
- Basic auto-scaling with threshold rules
- Anomaly detection with email alerts
- Cost dashboard with monthly reports
- Community support with forty-eight-hour response time
- Five user seats

## Professional Plan — One Thousand Four Hundred Ninety-Nine Dollars Per Month

Best for growing companies needing multi-cloud support and advanced features.

Includes everything in Starter, plus:

- Up to five hundred managed instances across two cloud providers
- Predictive auto-scaling with ML-based forecasting
- Advanced anomaly detection with Slack and PagerDuty integration
- Real-time cost optimization recommendations
- Custom alerting rules and escalation policies
- Priority support with four-hour response time
- Twenty-five user seats
- API access

## Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing

Best for large organizations with complex multi-cloud environments.

Includes everything in Professional, plus:

- Unlimited managed instances across all supported cloud providers
- Custom ML models trained on your specific workload patterns
- Dedicated anomaly detection tuning and threshold customization
- Full cost optimization automation with approval workflows
- White-glove onboarding and dedicated customer success manager
- Twenty-four-seven premium support with one-hour response SLA
- Unlimited user seats
- SSO and advanced RBAC
- Custom integrations and professional services
- Quarterly business reviews with optimization roadmap

## Volume Discounts

Annual billing is available at a fifteen percent discount on Starter and Professional plans. Multi-year commitments for Enterprise customers include additional savings — contact our sales team for details.
