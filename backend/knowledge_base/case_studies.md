# CloudScale AI — Customer Case Studies

## Case Study One: FinEdge Technologies

### Company

FinEdge Technologies is a mid-size fintech company processing over two million transactions daily across AWS and Azure.

### Challenge

FinEdge was experiencing frequent performance degradation during peak trading hours. Their manual scaling approach meant they were either over-provisioned by thirty percent during off-hours or under-provisioned during spikes, leading to latency issues that cost them an estimated fifty thousand dollars per incident in lost trades.

### Solution

CloudScale AI deployed its predictive auto-scaling engine, which learned FinEdge's traffic patterns within the first week. The anomaly detection system was integrated with their existing PagerDuty workflow.

### Results

- Sixty-seven percent reduction in scaling-related incidents within ninety days
- Twenty-eight percent decrease in monthly cloud spend from eliminating over-provisioning
- Average response time improved from two hundred forty milliseconds to ninety-five milliseconds during peak hours
- Zero unplanned downtime events in the six months since deployment
- Annual savings of approximately three hundred twenty thousand dollars

### Quote

"CloudScale AI basically paid for itself in the first month. The predictive scaling alone saves us more than we spend on the entire platform." — CTO, FinEdge Technologies

## Case Study Two: MedStream Health

### Company

MedStream Health is a healthcare SaaS platform serving over four hundred hospitals with patient data management and telehealth solutions.

### Challenge

As a HIPAA-regulated company, MedStream needed infrastructure monitoring that would not access or transmit patient data. Their multi-cloud setup across AWS and Google Cloud made unified monitoring extremely difficult, and their operations team was spending over sixty percent of their time on manual infrastructure management.

### Solution

CloudScale AI's agent-based architecture was ideal — it runs inside MedStream's VPC and only transmits aggregated metrics. The unified multi-cloud dashboard gave their team a single pane of glass across both providers.

### Results

- Operations team productivity improved by forty-five percent
- Anomaly detection caught a critical storage failure eleven minutes before it would have caused an outage affecting ninety-two hospitals
- Thirty-three percent reduction in cloud costs through right-sizing recommendations
- Full HIPAA compliance maintained throughout deployment
- Time to resolve infrastructure issues decreased from an average of forty-seven minutes to eight minutes

### Quote

"The fact that no patient data ever leaves our environment was the deciding factor. But the cost savings and anomaly detection made it a no-brainer." — VP of Engineering, MedStream Health

## Case Study Three: GameForge Studios

### Company

GameForge Studios is an online gaming company with twelve million monthly active users across three game titles.

### Challenge

Gaming workloads are notoriously unpredictable — a viral moment on social media could triple traffic in minutes. GameForge was spending over one hundred eighty thousand dollars per month on always-on capacity to handle potential spikes, with most of that capacity sitting idle eighty percent of the time.

### Solution

CloudScale AI's ML-based forecasting was trained on GameForge's historical traffic data, including event-driven spikes. The platform learned to correlate social media trends and in-game events with traffic surges.

### Results

- Forty-one percent reduction in monthly infrastructure costs
- Scaling response time decreased from eight minutes (manual) to under forty-five seconds (automated)
- Successfully handled a three-hundred-percent traffic surge during a viral event with zero player impact
- Saved approximately eight hundred eighty thousand dollars annually
- Player satisfaction scores improved by eighteen percent due to reduced latency

### Quote

"Our players never notice the infrastructure anymore. That is exactly what we want." — Head of Infrastructure, GameForge Studios
